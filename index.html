Major Non-Urban Land Use in US

For over 60 years, US Department of Agriculture Economic Research Service (USDA ERS) has been reporting major land use data for US land area which covers nearly 2.3 billion acres

Map below can be used to see % land use in each state for different non-urban land use categories (Cropland, Grassland, Forest, Special Use and Other) from the USDA ERS Major Land Uses (MLU) report

Category can be selected from the dropdown and hovering over the map gives the state information
